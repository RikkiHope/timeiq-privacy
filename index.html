TimeIQ Privacy Policy

Effective date: 27 May 2026

Overview

TimeIQ is a timer app for iOS and Android. It helps you create, run, save, export, and share timers. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how that information is handled.

Information Stored On Your Device

TimeIQ stores your timers, segments, repeat settings, reminder choices, app settings, favourites, and any saved local recordings on your device.

TimeIQ does not require a user account. TimeIQ does not run its own server to upload your timers, settings, or recordings.

Ads

The free version of TimeIQ may show Google AdMob ads. Google and its advertising partners may process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interactions, diagnostics, and related information for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

TimeIQ requests non-personalized ads where supported by the Google Mobile Ads SDK.

Purchases

TimeIQ may offer an optional ad-free purchase or subscription. Purchases are handled by the app store you use, such as Apple App Store or Google Play. TimeIQ does not directly collect or store your payment card details.

TimeIQ may receive purchase status information needed to unlock ad-free features. The app may store your ad-free entitlement state locally on your device.

Camera, Microphone, And Local Recordings

Camera and microphone access is optional. TimeIQ only asks for these permissions if you choose to use a recording feature.

Recordings are saved locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them. You can save or discard recordings after recording. Discarded pending recordings are deleted from the app's local storage.

Exporting And Sharing

If you export or share timers, files, or recordings, TimeIQ sends that content to the destination you choose, such as another app, a file location, a cloud provider, or a contact. Those third-party destinations handle your content under their own terms and privacy policies.

Data Retention And Deletion

Timers, settings, favourites, entitlement state, and saved recordings remain on your device until you edit them, delete them, clear app data, or uninstall TimeIQ.

You can delete timers inside the app. You can remove saved recordings from the location where they were saved. Uninstalling TimeIQ removes app-local data according to the normal storage behaviour of iOS or Android.

Children

TimeIQ is not specifically directed at children. The app is designed as a general timer tool for everyday timing, workouts, cooking, productivity, and similar uses.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact: timeiq.privacy@outlook.com
